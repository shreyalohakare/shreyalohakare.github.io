Sushi Master

4.5/5 Overall Rating
$$$ Price Range
Japanese Cuisine
No Reservations

Nestled in a charming corner of downtown, La Bella Italia has been serving authentic Italian cuisine for over two decades. As soon as you step through the rustic wooden doors, you're transported to a cozy trattoria in Rome, complete with checkered tablecloths and the warm aroma of fresh herbs and garlic.

Atmosphere

The restaurant's ambiance strikes the perfect balance between elegant and comfortable. Soft lighting from vintage chandeliers creates an intimate atmosphere, while the exposed brick walls and wooden beams add rustic charm. The gentle sounds of Italian music playing in the background complete the immersive experience.

Signature Dishes

Omakase Platter

★★★★★ (5/5)

A carefully selected array of seasonal fish, each piece of nigiri and sashimi is prepared with precision and respect for traditional Japanese techniques. Every bite bursts with freshness, from the buttery toro to the delicate snapper, making this a truly unforgettable tasting experience.

Price: $24

Spicy Tuna Roll

★★★★½ (4.5/5)

This classic roll takes on a new depth at Sushi Master, with exceptionally fresh tuna and a spicy mayo that’s balanced, not overpowering. The sushi rice is perfectly seasoned and rolled with expert precision.

Price: $38

Sake Selection

The sake menu is extensive, featuring both premium and lesser-known labels from across Japan. The knowledgeable staff is eager to guide guests through the offerings, suggesting ideal pairings to enhance the flavors of each dish.

Service

The staff's attention to detail and warm hospitality make dining here a truly memorable experience. They strike the perfect balance between attentiveness and allowing you to enjoy your meal in peace.

By Kenji Tanaka • October 18, 2024 • Japanese Cuisine •
